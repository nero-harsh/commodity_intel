As the traditional quant or the stock and price forcasting models are mainly dependent on the time data and past prices 
they dont consider the actual news and causes of the fluctuations of the prices .
They instead predict based on the affects of the events and news happening in the world after the market or the commodities are affected .

In this project I am trying to process major news related to specific commodities like gold , silver , lithium etc. while also 
including traditional time series forcasting methods as the base to understand the market and then use the news as a high weightage data
source and process them with LLM to get insights and scores for the commodity or raw material effectively forcast the rise or fall and by
severity of it in : low, med , high .

This doesn't have to be limited to specific commodities or data sources , can be used for stocks for futures mainly and even the data sources can be added
this is just my implementaion based on Chinese news as they dictate a major part of the commodities I chose .
